EdgeIQ
AI-powered betting analysis for TAB.com.au
1
Navigate to tab.com.au/racing or tab.com.au/sports and find a race or market.
2
Click the ⚡ tab on the right edge of the page to open the EdgeIQ panel.
3
Click "Analyse This Page" — EdgeIQ captures the screen and sends it to the AI.
4
Get a BET / WATCH / PASS / SKIP verdict with confidence score, edge (EV), runner breakdown, and probability table.
5
All scans auto-save to your EdgeIQ app → Scan History for outcome tracking.
🛡️
Stealth Mode: The panel runs inside a closed Shadow DOM. TAB's JavaScript cannot detect, query, or fingerprint the extension — it's completely invisible to the page.
